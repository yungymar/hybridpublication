The four narrators




 
Jordan - A young man, enjoying live to the fullest whilst taking a gap year. He parties alot, plays waterpolo at a high level, has a active dating & has a sales job on the side.

Ina - A cool mom, knows & rocks trends. Works in a chemical lab, likes to travel. Loves good weather and enjoys family time.

Leyla - Studies communication & marketing, loves to cook. Is a super energetic kind human, knows what she wants and how to get there. Loves to live in the vibrant city.

Cameron - Getting to know technology, is retired and quite bored so learning how to use tech. Not happy with the current political climate, loves teatime and formula 1. Has a cocker spaniel called Mario.

These narrators all have a dialect in the emoji language. In this scenario instant communication has been given only emoji keyboards, this means all of the social media & web based communication is in emoji language alone.


Obviously, this means the emoji language evolves, people have their own style of language, their own dialects. On the web part of the publication we will explore this part of the emoji language. 

As noted before you will read three narrators, responding or adding to the printed content. These narrators are the bitmojis depicted above.
